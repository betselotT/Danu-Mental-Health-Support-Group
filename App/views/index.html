About Us

Welcome to Danu Mental Health Support Group

At Danu, we believe in fostering a community that embraces mental wellness and supports individuals on their journey to healing. Named after the amharic word ዳኑ, getting healed after being taken care of, our support group embodies these values of care, understanding, and resilience.

Mission

Our Mission Our mission at Danu is simple yet profound: to create a safe, inclusive space where individuals facing mental health challenges can find solace, understanding, and hope. We are committed to destigmatizing mental health issues and fostering a culture of empathy, support, and empowerment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Perhaps some of your questions are answered below

Are the sessions confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is a cornerstone of our services. All discussions and information shared during our sessions are kept strictly confidential.

How can I join a support group session?

You can join our support group sessions by reaching out to us via our contact form or by scheduling an appointment.

Are your counselors licensed professionals?

Yes, our counselors and mental health professionals are qualified and licensed practitioners with expertise in various areas of mental health.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by filling out our appointment request form on the website.
